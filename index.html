How a project actually runs

a.

Start with the problem, not the mood board

Before any visuals, I want to understand what the brand needs to do in the world — who it needs to convince, and of what.

b.

Design the system, not just the mark

A logo is the smallest part of an identity. Type, color, spacing rules, and tone of voice are what make it usable by a whole team.

c.

Hand off something you can actually run

Every project ships with a working guideline doc and source files — not a PDF that gets opened once and forgotten.
